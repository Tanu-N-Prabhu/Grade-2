# Grade 2 English Quiz – Set 4 (1 Hour)

## Section A: Grammar

1. Circle the adjective:  
   *The small puppy is sleeping.*

2. Choose the correct verb:  
   The girls ___ in the park.  
   (play / plays / playing)

3. Pick the correct punctuation mark:  
   What is your name ___  
   (? / ! / .)

## Section B: Sentence Writing

4. Write a sentence using the word: **morning**

5. Fix the sentence:  
   *we are going to the zoo*

## Section C: Reading Comprehension

Read the passage:

*"Tom has a new bike. It is bright yellow and very fast. He rides it every day after school, but he never goes too far."*

6. What color is Tom’s bike?

7. When does Tom ride his bike?

## Section D: Word Skills

8. Write two rhyming words for: **sun**

9. Write the opposite of: **slow**

10. Unscramble the word: **K O O B**

## Answer Key

1. small  
2. play  
3. ?  
4. (Any correct sentence)  
5. We are going to the zoo.  
6. Yellow  
7. After school  
8. (Examples: fun, run, bun)  
9. fast  
10. book
