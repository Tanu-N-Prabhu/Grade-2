# Grade 2 English Test – Advanced Level (1 Hour)

---

## Section A: Grammar & Language (Hard)

1. Circle the adjective **and** underline the noun in the sentence:  
   *The clever fox ran into the dark forest.*

2. Choose the correct verb and rewrite the sentence:  
   The children ___ playing quietly.  
   (is / are)

3. Fill in the blanks using the correct words:  
   a) She ___ (has / have) two pencils.  
   b) They ___ (is / are) late for school.

4. Choose the correct sentence:
   - a) my brother likes pizza  
   - b) My brother like pizza.  
   - c) My brother likes pizza.

5. Add the correct punctuation:  
   what time is it

---

## Section B: Sentence Building (Hard)

6. Rearrange the words to make a correct sentence:  
   *after / park / the / school / went / we / to*

7. Combine the two sentences into one:  
   *The dog is small. The dog is friendly.*

8. Write a sentence using **both** words:  
   **because** and **happy**

---

## Section C: Reading Comprehension (Hard)

Read the passage carefully:

*"Every morning, Noah wakes up early to help his mother. He packs his lunch, feeds the dog, and checks his school bag. One day, he forgot his homework, so he felt worried all morning."*

9. Why does Noah wake up early?

10. Name **two** things Noah does in the morning.

11. How does Noah feel when he forgets his homework?

12. Why do you think Noah felt that way? (Write one sentence.)

---

## Section D: Vocabulary & Word Skills (Hard)

13. Write a synonym for: **big**

14. Write an antonym for: **early**

15. Write two describing words for: **morning**

16. Unscramble the word:  
    **E R A C H T E**

---

## Section E: Writing (Challenge)

17. Write **4–5 sentences** about your morning routine.  
   Use:
   - At least **one adjective**
   - At least **one action word**
   - Correct punctuation
