## Section A: Grammar & Language (Advanced)

1. Circle the adjective and underline the noun:  
   *The tired boy carried a heavy backpack.*

2. Choose the correct word and rewrite the sentence:  
   She ___ finished her work before dinner.  
   (has / have)

3. Fill in the blanks correctly:  
   a) The cat ___ sleeping on the chair. (is / are)  
   b) We ___ going to the library today. (is / are)

4. Fix the sentence:  
   *after lunch we played outside*

5. Add the correct punctuation:  
   where did you put my book

## Section B: Sentence Thinking (Advanced)

6. Rearrange the words to make a meaningful sentence:  
   *quietly / class / the / listened / students*

7. Join the sentences using **and** or **because**:  
   *It was raining. We stayed inside.*

8. Write a sentence using **before** and **after**.

## Section C: Reading Comprehension (Hard)

Read the passage carefully:

*"On Saturday morning, Maya planned to visit the park. She packed her bag with water and snacks. When she stepped outside, the sky turned dark and rain began to fall. Maya felt disappointed, but she decided to read her favorite book instead."*

9. What did Maya plan to do on Saturday morning?

10. Why did Maya pack her bag?

11. What problem did Maya face?

12. How did Maya solve her problem?

13. How do you think Maya felt at the end? Explain in one sentence.
