# Grade 2 English Quiz – Set 6 (1 Hour)

## Section A: Grammar

1. Circle the adjective:  
   *The bright sun is shining.*

2. Choose the correct verb:  
   They ___ in the garden.  
   (play / plays / playing)

3. Fill in the blank with the correct pronoun:  
   ___ am reading a book. (I / He / She)

## Section B: Sentence Writing

4. Write a sentence using the word: **school**

5. Fix the sentence:  
   *tommy likes to draw pictures*

## Section C: Reading Comprehension

Read the passage:

*"Lucy loves flowers. Every day she waters her garden. Her favorite flower is a red rose."*

6. Who waters the garden?

7. What is Lucy’s favorite flower?

## Section D: Word Skills

8. Write two rhyming words for: **cat**

9. Write the opposite of: **hot**

10. Unscramble the word: **E R U N**
  
## Answer Key

1. bright  
2. play  
3. I  
4. (Any correct sentence)  
5. Tommy likes to draw pictures.  
6. Lucy  
7. Red rose  
8. (Examples: hat, mat, bat)  
9. cold  
10. run
