## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Understand what an opinion is
- Give a reason for their opinion
- Write simple opinion sentences
- Share their ideas clearly

---

## Alberta ELA Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **1.1 Discover and Explore:** Share ideas and preferences
- **2.2 Attend to Conventions:** Use simple sentence structure
- **3.1 Plan and Focus:** Write about a topic
- **4.1 Enhance the Clarity of Communication:** Express ideas clearly

---

## What is an Opinion?

An opinion tells what you think or feel.

Example:

"I like rainy days because I can jump in puddles."

---

## Model Writing

### Topic: My Favorite Fruit

I like apples.  
They are sweet and crunchy.  
I like apples because they taste good.

---

## Writing Activity

### Part 1: Choose One

Pick one topic:

- Favorite food
- Favorite animal
- Favorite game
- Favorite place

---

### Part 2: Write Your Opinion

Use this structure:

1. My favorite __________ is __________.
2. I like it because __________.
3. It makes me feel __________.

---

### Part 3: Add One More Idea

Write one more sentence:

I also like it because __________.

---

## Optional Drawing

Draw your favorite thing.

---

## Sharing

Students read one sentence aloud.

---

## Assessment (Informal)

- States an opinion
- Gives a reason
- Completes sentences

---

## Differentiation

- **Support:** Sentence starters on board
- **Extension:** Write one sentence that begins with:

"I think __________ is the best because __________."
