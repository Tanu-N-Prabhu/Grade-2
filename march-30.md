# Grade 2 English – 1 Hour Lesson Plan  
**Curriculum Style:** Alberta Board of Education  
**Focus:** Reading, Vocabulary, Writing, and Speaking  
**Theme:** Animals and Describing Words  

---

# Learning Objectives
By the end of this session, students will be able to:

- Read a short passage with understanding  
- Identify describing words (adjectives)  
- Answer simple comprehension questions  
- Write 3–4 simple sentences about an animal  
- Speak about their favorite animal

---

# Materials Needed

- Whiteboard or notebook  
- Pencil  
- Printed worksheet (optional)  
- Picture of animals (optional)

---

# Lesson Structure (60 Minutes)

| Time | Activity |
|-----|------|
| 10 min | Warm-up conversation |
| 15 min | Reading activity |
| 10 min | Vocabulary (Describing Words) |
| 15 min | Writing practice |
| 10 min | Speaking activity |

---

# 1. Warm-Up (10 Minutes)

Ask students simple questions:

- What is your favorite animal?
- Do you have a pet?
- What sound does a dog make?
- What animal lives in the jungle?

Teacher can write answers on the board.

Example:

Dog → **barks**  
Cat → **meows**  
Cow → **moos**

---

# 2. Reading Activity (15 Minutes)

### Short Story

**The Little Rabbit**

A little rabbit lives in a green forest.  
The rabbit is small and white.  
It has long ears and a fluffy tail.  
The rabbit likes to eat carrots.  
Every morning, it hops happily in the grass.

---

# 3. Reading Questions

Ask students to answer:

1. Where does the rabbit live?  
2. What color is the rabbit?  
3. What does the rabbit like to eat?  
4. What does the rabbit do in the morning?

---

# 4. Vocabulary – Describing Words (10 Minutes)

Explain:

**Describing words tell us more about something.**

Examples:

| Word | Example |
|-----|------|
| big | big elephant |
| small | small rabbit |
| fast | fast tiger |
| fluffy | fluffy tail |
| happy | happy dog |

---

# Activity

Circle the describing word.

1. The **big** elephant walks slowly.  
2. The **small** bird sings.  
3. The **fast** cheetah runs.  

---

# 5. Writing Practice (15 Minutes)

### Write About Your Favorite Animal

Fill in the blanks.

My favorite animal is a __________.  
It is __________.  
It lives in __________.  
It likes to eat __________.

---

### Example

My favorite animal is a **dog**.  
It is **friendly**.  
It lives in **my house**.  
It likes to eat **bones**.

---

# 6. Speaking Activity (10 Minutes)

Each student speaks for 20–30 seconds.

Prompt:

Tell the class about your favorite animal.

Students should say:

- Animal name  
- Color  
- What it eats  
- One describing word

Example:

"My favorite animal is a cat.  
It is black and soft.  
It likes to eat fish.  
It is very playful."

---

# Wrap-Up

Review with students:

- What is a describing word?
- What animal did you write about?
- What did the rabbit eat?

Encourage students to draw their favorite animal at home and write 2 sentences about it.

---

# Homework

Draw your favorite animal and write:

1. Its name  
2. One describing word  
3. What it eats
