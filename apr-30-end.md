# Grade 2 English – Fun Lesson  
**Topic:** Opposite Words  
**Time:** 1 Hour  

---

# Learning Goals

Students will:

- Learn common opposite words
- Match words with their opposites
- Use opposite words in sentences
- Speak using opposite words

---

# Warm-Up (10 min)

Teacher says a word.  
Students say the opposite.

- big → small
- hot → cold
- up → down
- happy → sad

---

# What Are Opposites?

Opposites are words with opposite meanings.

Example:

- day and night
- open and close
- fast and slow

---

# Opposite Word List

| Word | Opposite |
|------|----------|
| big | small |
| tall | short |
| hot | cold |
| happy | sad |
| fast | slow |
| open | close |
| full | empty |
| clean | dirty |

---

# Activity 1: Match the Opposites (15 min)

Draw lines to match:

big          cold  
hot          small  
happy        sad  
fast         slow  

---

# Activity 2: Fill in the Blank (15 min)

Choose the correct opposite word.

1. The elephant is big. The mouse is ______.  
2. Ice is cold. Soup is ______.  
3. The rabbit is fast. The turtle is ______.  
4. The glass is full. Now it is ______.  

---

# Activity 3: Sentence Time (10 min)

Complete the sentences.

1. The tree is tall, but the bush is ______.  
2. My room is clean, but my shoes are ______.  
3. I feel happy when I play. I feel ______ when I am sick.  

---

# Speaking Activity (5 min)

Say one pair of opposite words.

Example:

"Up and down."

Or use them in a sentence:

"The kite goes up and comes down."

---

# Wrap-Up

Ask:

- What is the opposite of big?
- What is the opposite of open?
- What is the opposite of happy?

---

# Homework

Write 5 pairs of opposite words.

Example:

- big – small
- hot – cold
