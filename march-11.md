# Grade 2 Mathematics
**Alberta Board of Education**  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Focus:** Addition and Subtraction Strategies

---

## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Use mental math to add and subtract numbers
- Break numbers into tens and ones
- Solve addition and subtraction problems within 100
- Explain how they found their answers

---

## Alberta Math Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **Number Sense:** Work with numbers up to 100
- **Operations:** Use strategies to add and subtract
- **Mathematical Thinking:** Explain reasoning

---

## Strategy 1: Break Apart Numbers

Break numbers into tens and ones.

Example:

34 + 25

34 = 30 + 4  
25 = 20 + 5  

Add tens:  
30 + 20 = 50  

Add ones:  
4 + 5 = 9  

Final Answer:  
50 + 9 = **59**

---

## Strategy 2: Count Back for Subtraction

Example:

42 − 6

42 → 41 → 40 → 39 → 38 → 37 → 36  

Answer: **36**
