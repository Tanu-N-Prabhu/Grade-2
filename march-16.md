# Grade 2 English Language Arts (ELA)
**Alberta Board of Education**  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Focus:** Sentence Building and Writing

---

## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Understand what makes a complete sentence
- Arrange words to form correct sentences
- Write simple sentences about a topic
- Use capital letters and periods correctly

---

## Alberta ELA Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **1.1 Discover and Explore:** Share ideas through speaking and writing
- **2.2 Attend to Conventions:** Use capital letters and punctuation
- **3.1 Plan and Focus:** Write simple sentences about a topic
- **4.1 Enhance the Clarity of Communication:** Organize ideas clearly

---

## What is a Sentence?

A sentence:
- Starts with a **capital letter**
- Tells a **complete idea**
- Ends with a **period**

Example:

The dog runs fast.

---

## Assessment (Informal)

- Uses capital letters and periods
- Forms complete sentences
- Participates in writing activities

---

## Differentiation

**Support**
Provide sentence starters:
- I like __________.
- I see __________.
- My favorite place is __________.

**Extension**
Write **5 sentences** about your favorite place instead of 3.
