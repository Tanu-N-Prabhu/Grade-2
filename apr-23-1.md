# Grade 2 Math – Geometry (Simple Lesson)  
**Topic:** 2D Shapes  
**Time:** 1 Hour
```

---

# Learning

Students will:

- Name basic shapes  
- Count sides and corners  
- Draw simple shapes  

---

# Shapes to Learn

### Circle
- No sides  
- No corners  

### Triangle
- 3 sides  
- 3 corners  

### Square
- 4 equal sides  
- 4 corners  

### Rectangle
- 4 sides  
- 4 corners  

---

# Warm-Up (10 min)

Ask:

- What shape is a clock?  
- What shape is a book?  

---

# Activity 1 (15 min)

Count sides:

Triangle → ___ sides  
Square → ___ sides  
Rectangle → ___ sides  

---

# Activity 2 (15 min)

Fill in the blanks:

1. A circle has ___ sides  
2. A triangle has ___ corners  
3. A square has ___ sides  
4. A rectangle has ___ corners  

---

# Activity 3 (10 min)

Match:

Circle → ______  
Triangle → ______  
Square → ______  
Rectangle → ______  

(Teacher can draw shapes on board)

---

# Drawing (10 min)

Draw:

- 1 circle  
- 1 triangle  
- 1 square  
- 1 rectangle  

---

# Speaking (5 min)

Say:

"This is a ______."

---

# Homework

Draw 3 shapes and write their names.
