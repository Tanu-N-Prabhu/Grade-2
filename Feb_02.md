### Talk About Your Day

## üìñ 2. Reading & Comprehension (15 Minutes)

### Short Story: *My Busy Day*

> I wake up early in the morning.  
> I brush my teeth and eat breakfast.  
> I go to school with my bag.  
> I learn and play with my friends.  
> I come home and rest.

---

### üß† Comprehension Questions

3. Where does the child go?  
4. Who does the child play with?  
5. What does the child do at the end of the day?

## üî§ 3. Grammar & Vocabulary (15 Minutes)

**Activity:**  
Say one sentence using the word **school** or **friends**.

---

### ‚úèÔ∏è Grammar Focus: Nouns (Naming Words)

A **noun** is a naming word.  
It names a **person**, **place**, or **thing**.

**Examples:**
- Person: boy, teacher  
- Place: school, park  
- Thing: bag, book  

**Circle the nouns:**

1. The **boy** has a **bag**.  
2. I go to **school**.  
3. My **friends** play with a **ball**.

## ‚úçÔ∏è 4. Writing Practice (15 Minutes)

### Sentence Writing

Write **4 sentences** about your day.

Use these clues:
- morning  
- school  
- friends  
- home  

**Example:**
> I go to school in the morning.

**Writing Rules:**
- Capital letter at the start  
- Period at the end
