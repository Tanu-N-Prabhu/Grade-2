# Grade 2 English Language Arts (ELA)
**Alberta Board of Education**  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Focus:** Writing – How-To (Procedural Writing)

---

## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Understand how-to writing
- Write steps in the correct order
- Use sequencing words
- Explain how to do something

---

## Alberta ELA Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **1.1 Discover and Explore:** Share ideas and experiences
- **2.2 Attend to Conventions:** Use simple sentences
- **3.1 Plan and Focus:** Write about a topic with purpose
- **4.1 Enhance the Clarity of Communication:** Organize ideas clearly

---

## What is How-To Writing?

How-to writing explains how to do something.

We use ordering words like:

- First
- Next
- Then
- Last

---

## Model Text

### *How to Wash Your Hands*

First, turn on the water.  
Next, put soap on your hands.  
Then, rub your hands together.  
Last, rinse your hands and dry them.

---

## Writing Activity

### Part 1: Choose One Topic

Write how to:

- Tie your shoes  
- Draw a house  
- Make a sandwich  
- Get ready for school  

---

### Part 2: Write the Steps

Use sequencing words.

1. First, ___________________________.
2. Next, ___________________________.
3. Then, ___________________________.
4. Last, ___________________________.

---

### Part 3: Add a Title

How to ___________________________

---

## Optional Drawing

Draw one step from your how-to writing.

---

## Sharing

Students read their steps aloud.

---

## Assessment (Informal)

- Uses sequencing words
- Steps are in order
- Sentences are clear

---

## Differentiation

- **Support:** Provide step prompts orally
- **Extension:** Add one extra step using:

"Finally, ___________________________."
