# Grade 2 Mathematics
**Alberta Board of Education**  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Focus:** Place Value – Tens and Ones

---

## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Understand that numbers are made of tens and ones
- Break numbers into tens and ones
- Write numbers using place value
- Practice simple number decomposition

---

## Alberta Math Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **Number Sense:** Understand numbers to 100
- **Represent Numbers:** Use tens and ones to represent numbers
- **Mathematical Communication:** Explain thinking using numbers and words

---

## Understanding Place Value

Numbers have different place values.

Example:

42 = 4 tens + 2 ones

| Number | Tens | Ones |
|------|------|------|
| 34 | 3 | 4 |
| 58 | 5 | 8 |
| 71 | 7 | 1 |

- Draw 3 tens rods

---

## Assessment (Informal)

- Correctly identifies tens and ones
- Completes number building activities
- Participates in discussion

---

## Differentiation

**Support**
- Use physical objects (sticks or blocks) to show tens and ones

**Extension**
- Write two numbers that have **4 tens**
