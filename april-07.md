# Grade 2 English – 1 Hour Lesson Plan  
**Curriculum Style:** Alberta Board of Education  
**Focus:** Reading, Phonics, Vocabulary, Writing  
**Theme:** Long Vowel Sounds (ai, ee, oa)

---

# Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

- Recognize common long vowel sounds  
- Read words containing **ai, ee, and oa**  
- Read and understand a short passage  
- Identify long vowel words in a sentence  
- Write simple sentences using new vocabulary

---

# Materials Needed

- Whiteboard or notebook  
- Pencil  
- Flashcards (optional)  
- Word list on board

---

# Lesson Plan (60 Minutes)

| Time | Activity |
|------|---------|
| 10 min | Warm-up phonics review |
| 15 min | Learn long vowel sounds |
| 15 min | Reading passage |
| 10 min | Word activity |
| 10 min | Writing practice |

---

# 1. Warm-Up (10 Minutes)

Review basic vowel sounds.

Write on the board:

A E I O U

Ask students to read them together.

Explain:

Sometimes vowels make **long sounds**.

Examples:

A → **rain**  
E → **tree**  
O → **boat**

Say the words together aloud.

---

# 2. Long Vowel Sounds (15 Minutes)

### ai Sound

Examples:

rain  
train  
paint  
tail

Example sentence:

The **train** runs in the **rain**.

---

### ee Sound

Examples:

tree  
bee  
see  
green

Example sentence:

I can **see** a **green tree**.

---

### oa Sound

Examples:

boat  
road  
coat  
goat

Example sentence:

The **goat** walks on the **road**.

---

# 3. Reading Passage (15 Minutes)

### The Boat Ride

Sam and Lee see a big boat.  
The boat floats on the lake.  
They see a green tree near the road.  
A goat stands by the tree.  
Sam and Lee smile and wave.

---

# 4. Reading Questions

Ask students:

1. Who saw the boat?  
2. Where is the boat?  
3. What animal stands near the tree?  
4. What did Sam and Lee do?

Students may answer verbally or write short responses.

---

# 5. Word Activity (10 Minutes)

Circle the long vowel words.

1. The **bee** sits on a flower.  
2. The **train** moves fast.  
3. The **goat** eats grass.  
4. I see a **green** leaf.

Students identify:

bee  
train  
goat  
green

---

# 6. Writing Practice (10 Minutes)

Write **3 sentences** using these words:

tree  
rain  
boat  
goat

Example:

The **goat** eats grass.  
I see a tall **tree**.  
The **boat** floats on the lake.

Encourage correct **capital letters and periods**.

---

# Speaking Practice

Students read one sentence aloud using a word from today’s lesson.

Example:

"I see a green tree."

---

# Wrap-Up

Review the sounds learned today:

ai  
ee  
oa

Ask students to say one word for each sound.

Example:

ai → rain  
ee → bee  
oa → boat

---

# Homework

Write **4 words** for each sound.

ai words: __________  
ee words: __________  
oa words: __________

Draw a picture of one word and label it.
