# Grade 2 English Language Arts (ELA)
**Alberta Board of Education**  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Topic:** Reading Comprehension & Sequencing Events

---

## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Read and understand a short story
- Identify the order of events (first, next, then, last)
- Answer comprehension questions
- Write sentences using sequencing words

---

## Alberta ELA Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **1.1 Discover and Explore:** Respond to stories read independently
- **2.1 Use Strategies and Cues:** Use meaning cues and prior knowledge
- **3.1 Plan and Focus:** Write short, clear sentences
- **4.1 Enhance the Clarity of Communication:** Organize ideas in order

---

## Reading Text

### *Max Builds a Birdhouse*

Max wanted to build a birdhouse for his backyard. First, he asked his dad for some wood and nails. His dad showed him how to use a small hammer safely.

Next, Max carefully nailed the pieces of wood together. Then, he painted the birdhouse bright yellow. He let it dry in the sun.

Last, Max hung the birdhouse on a tree branch. The next morning, a small bird sat on it. Max felt proud of his hard work.

---

## Reading Comprehension

1. What did Max want to build?
2. Who helped Max?
3. What color did Max paint the birdhouse?
4. Where did Max put the birdhouse?
5. How did Max feel at the end?

---

## Sequencing Activity

Put the events in order by numbering them 1–4.

___ Max painted the birdhouse yellow.  
___ Max hung the birdhouse on a tree.  
___ Max nailed the wood pieces together.  
___ Max asked his dad for wood and nails.

---

## Writing Activity

Write four sentences using sequencing words.

1. First, ______________________________.
2. Next, ______________________________.
3. Then, ______________________________.
4. Last, ______________________________.

**Optional Drawing:**  
Draw Max’s yellow birdhouse in the tree.

---

## Review & Discussion

- What happened first?
- What happened after Max painted the birdhouse?
- Why was Max proud?

---

## Assessment (Informal)

- Correct sequencing of events
- Complete written sentences
- Participation in discussion

---

## Differentiation

- **Support:** Provide event cards for students to arrange physically
- **Extension:** Write 2–3 sentences about something you built or made, using sequencing words
