# Grade 2 English Language Arts (ELA)
**Alberta Board of Education**  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Focus:** Paragraph Writing (My Day)

---

## Learning Objectives
Students will:
- Understand what a paragraph is
- Write 3–5 sentences on one topic
- Use sequencing words (First, Next, Then, Last)
- Write clear and complete sentences

---

## Alberta ELA Outcomes (Grade 2)

- **1.1 Discover and Explore:** Share personal experiences
- **2.2 Attend to Conventions:** Use correct sentence structure
- **3.1 Plan and Focus:** Write about a topic with simple organization
- **4.1 Enhance the Clarity of Communication:** Organize ideas clearly

---

## What is a Paragraph?

A paragraph is a group of sentences about one idea.

Example:

First, I wake up in the morning.  
Next, I brush my teeth.  
Then, I eat my breakfast.  
Last, I go to school.

---

## Activity 1: Read and Understand

Read the paragraph above.

Answer:

2. What does the child do last? __________________

## Differentiation

**Support**

Provide sentence starters and allow oral responses.

**Extension**

Write a paragraph with **5–6 sentences** and add more details.
