# Grade 2 Math – Fun Lesson  
**Topic:** Patterns Around Us  
**Time:** 1 Hour  

---

# Learning Goals

Students will:

- Recognize patterns
- Complete simple patterns
- Create their own patterns
- Find patterns in everyday life

---

# Warm-Up (10 min)

Clap a pattern:

Clap, Tap, Clap, Tap

Ask students to repeat.

Then try:

Jump, Jump, Clap, Jump, Jump, Clap

---

# What Is a Pattern?

A pattern is something that repeats.

Example:

🔴 🔵 🔴 🔵 🔴 🔵

Red, Blue, Red, Blue

---

# Activity 1: Complete the Pattern (15 min)

1. ⭐ ⚪ ⭐ ⚪ ⭐ ___  
2. 🔺 🔵 🔺 🔵 ___ ___  
3. 🍎 🍌 🍎 🍌 🍎 ___  
4. 2, 4, 2, 4, ___, ___  

---

# Activity 2: Find the Rule (10 min)

What comes next?

1. 5, 10, 5, 10, ___  
2. 1, 2, 3, 1, 2, 3, ___  
3. 🟩 🟨 🟩 🟨 🟩 ___  

---

# Activity 3: Make Your Own Pattern (15 min)

Create a pattern using:

- Shapes
- Colors
- Numbers

Example:

Circle, Square, Circle, Square

Or

1, 3, 1, 3, 1, 3

---

# Pattern Hunt (5 min)

Look around the classroom.

Can you find a pattern on:

- The floor?
- Your clothes?
- A book cover?

---

# Speaking Activity (5 min)

Say your pattern aloud.

Example:

"My pattern is red, blue, red, blue."

---

# Homework

Draw and color one repeating pattern.

Example ideas:

- Shape pattern
- Color pattern
- Number pattern
