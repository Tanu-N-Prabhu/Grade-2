```md id="9g114c"
# Grade 2 Math – Geometry (Simple Lesson)  
**Topic:** 3D Shapes  
**Time:** 1 Hour  

---

# Learning

Students will:

- Name 3D shapes  
- Match shapes with real objects  
- Draw simple shapes  

---

# Shapes to Learn

### Sphere
- Round  
- Example: ball  

### Cube
- Box shape  
- Example: dice  

### Cylinder
- Round with flat top and bottom  
- Example: can  

### Cone
- Pointy top  
- Example: ice cream cone  

---

# Warm-Up (10 min)

Ask:

- What shape is a ball?  
- What shape is a box?  

---

# Activity 1 (15 min)

Match:

Ball → ______  
Box → ______  
Can → ______  
Ice cream → ______  

---

# Activity 2 (15 min)

Circle the shapes that **roll**:

Sphere  
Cube  
Cylinder  
Cone  

---

# Drawing (10 min)

Draw:

- 1 sphere  
- 1 cube  
- 1 cylinder  
- 1 cone  

---

# Speaking (5–10 min)

Say one sentence:

"This is a ______."

---

# Homework

Draw 2 shapes and write their names.
```
