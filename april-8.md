# Grade 2 English – 1 Hour Lesson Plan  
**Curriculum Style:** Alberta Board of Education  
**Focus:** Reading, Grammar, Vocabulary, Writing  
**Theme:** Action Words (Verbs)

---

# Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

- Understand what **action words (verbs)** are  
- Identify verbs in sentences  
- Read a short passage and find action words  
- Use verbs to write simple sentences  
- Speak using action words

---

# Materials Needed

- Whiteboard or notebook  
- Pencil  
- Action word flashcards (optional)

---

# Lesson Plan (60 Minutes)

| Time | Activity |
|------|---------|
| 10 min | Warm-up activity |
| 15 min | Grammar lesson (Action Words) |
| 15 min | Reading passage |
| 10 min | Verb identification activity |
| 10 min | Writing practice |

---

# 1. Warm-Up Activity (10 Minutes)

Ask students to act out simple actions.

Teacher says a word, students perform it.

Examples:

jump  
run  
clap  
sit  
stand

Ask:

What are we doing?

Explain:

These are **action words**.

---

# 2. Grammar Lesson – Action Words (15 Minutes)

Explain:

A **verb** is an **action word**.  
It tells us **what someone or something is doing**.

Examples:

| Sentence | Action Word |
|--------|-------------|
| The boy **runs** fast. | runs |
| The bird **flies** in the sky. | flies |
| The girl **reads** a book. | reads |
| The dog **barks** loudly. | barks |

---

# 3. Reading Passage (15 Minutes)

### The Busy Day

Maya wakes up early.  
She **brushes** her teeth.  
She **eats** breakfast with her family.  
Then she **walks** to school.  
At school, Maya **reads**, **writes**, and **plays** with her friends.

---

# 4. Reading Questions

Ask students:

1. What does Maya do first in the morning?  
2. What does she eat?  
3. How does she go to school?  
4. What does she do at school?

---

# 5. Verb Identification Activity (10 Minutes)

Underline the action words.

1. The cat **runs** fast.  
2. The baby **cries** loudly.  
3. The children **play** in the park.  
4. The teacher **writes** on the board.

Students underline:

runs  
cries  
play  
writes

---

# 6. Fill in the Blank

Choose the correct action word.

(run, eat, read, jump)

1. I ______ a book.  
2. The frog can ______ high.  
3. We ______ lunch at noon.  
4. The dog can ______ fast.

---

# 7. Writing Practice (10 Minutes)

Write **4 sentences** using action words.

Example:

I **run** in the park.  
I **read** a story.  
I **eat** an apple.  
I **play** with my friends.

Encourage students to use:

- Capital letters  
- Periods at the end

---

# Speaking Activity

Students say one sentence using an action word.

Example:

"I jump high."  
"I read books."

---

# Wrap-Up

Review with students:

- What is a verb?  
- Give one action word.

Examples students may say:

run  
jump  
read  
eat  
play

---

# Homework

Write **5 action words** you do every day.

Example:

wake  
eat  
play  
read  
sleep
